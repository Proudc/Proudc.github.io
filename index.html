    • About Me

    I joined the College of Software, Zhejiang University, as an Assistant Professor in October 2024. I received my Bachelor's degree from Tianjin University in 2019 and my Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in 2024. My primary research interests focus on sequence representation learning and software-hardware performance optimization. I am actively recruiting Master's and Ph.D. students for my research team. Interested students are welcome to contact me at changzhihao@zju.edu.cn.
